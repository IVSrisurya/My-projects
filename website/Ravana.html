About

Ravana is the mythical multi-headed demon-king of Lanka in Hindu mythology. With ten heads and twenty arms, Ravana could become any form he wished. Representing the very essence of evil, he famously fought and ultimately lost a series of epic battles against the hero Rama, seventh avatar of Vishnu.

Ravana had a formidable appearance together with his ten heads (and so he's also referred to as Dasakantha and Panktigriva) and twenty arms.
